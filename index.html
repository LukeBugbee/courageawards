About the Cicero Institute

The Cicero Institute is a nonpartisan group of policy innovators with deep experience in government, legislating, research, technology, and entrepreneurship. Together, we incubate, develop, and fight for policies that apply the principles of a free society and solve problems at the state level, impacting millions of Americans. We are fighting back against government dysfunction, holding unaccountable bureaucrats to account, and preserving the fire of liberty for the future. Visit CiceroInstitute.org

About the Cicero Courage Awards

We founded the Cicero Institute to pursue a new vision of public policy in America — to use policy innovation to make public institutions more transparent, functional, and accountable to the public. In just a few short years, we’ve made waves in policy areas like homelessness, healthcare, regulation, public safety, and others, with dozens of new laws on the books reflecting that vision. In our fight against special interests, cronyism, and unaccountable bureaucrats, we’ve partnered with courageous citizen legislators to get reforms over the line. Our Cicero Courage Awards will recognize those leaders who have shown principle and courage when it matters most.
